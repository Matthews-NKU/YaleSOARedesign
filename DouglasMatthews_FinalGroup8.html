Note: Faculty appointments in each area of graduate study correspond to varying levels of engagement at the School of Art. This page reflects the faculty and staff at the Yale School of Art during the 2024-2025 academic year. Full-Time Faculty: On campus regularly each week for courses, critiques, studio visits, advising and other service. Full Time faculty also includes the Directors and Assistant Directors for each area of study. Part-Time Faculty: Teaching or co-teaching a weekly course or courses Visiting Critics: On campus occasionally for studio/desk visits or workshops
